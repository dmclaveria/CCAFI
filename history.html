CCAFI was launched through a motorcade around the city to signal its opening of Summer Class tutorial on April 7, 2013. It had its Final opening of classes June 6, 2013 for SY 2013-2014.

CCAFI purpose is to see children, being influenced and trained in the ways of the Lord, growing in the wisdom of God and striving for excellence not just academically but in all aspects of their lives having the Spirit of God in them.

CCAFI also aims to contribute to the transformation of the community by giving the children of indigent family scholarships, all-expense free quality education and ushering them into the realities of God. By doing so, deliquent children are reduced thus problems of the community are lessen.

As a whole, we at Charis Christian Academy focus on the holistic development of every child.
